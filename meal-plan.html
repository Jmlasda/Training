Prep Day 1 Sunday — ~45 min
Do this after Sunday dinner while kitchen is already dirty
1
Hard boil 8 eggsCover with cold water, bring to boil, 10 min, ice bath. Peeled and ready for Mon–Wed snacks and lunches.
2
Cook a big batch of white rice (4–5 cups dry)Covers Sunday dinner, Monday lunch bowl, and extra. Rice cooker or pot.
3
Season and roast extra chicken thighs (4–6 pieces)Same sheet pan as dinner. Extra portions go in fridge for Monday lunch bowls — just reheat.
4
Wash and portion all berriesRinse strawberries, raspberries, blueberries, blackberries. Store in containers — grab-and-go all week.
5
Slice tri-color peppersHalf for Sunday roasting, half stored raw for stir-fry Friday and pizza Tuesday.
Prep Day 2 Tuesday — ~20 min
Pizza night is mostly hands-off — use that time to prep
1
Make extra pizza dough or buy 2 packagesOne for tonight, one to freeze for next week. Store-bought dough is completely fine.
2
Cook ground turkey for Thursday taco bowlsBrown with cumin, garlic, chili powder, salt. Cool and refrigerate — Thursday dinner is 10 min reheat.
3
Cook a batch of rice or quinoaCovers Wednesday dinner (quinoa) and Thursday taco bowls (rice). Cook both if you have two pots.
Prep Day 3 Thursday — ~15 min
Taco bowl dinner is already prepped — use the extra time
1
Marinate chicken for Friday stir-frySlice chicken breast thin. Toss in soy sauce, sesame oil, garlic, ginger. Refrigerate overnight — 5 min Friday night cook.
2
Portion Greek yogurt into jars5–6 single-serve portions in mason jars or containers. Grab-and-go for Fri–Sun breakfasts and snacks.
